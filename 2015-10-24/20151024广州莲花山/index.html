20151024广州莲花山游记

2015年10月24日，小生出差广州处理某金融项目时，顺道游览了广州莲花山。
莲花山嘛，顾名思义，当然是遍地莲花了，只是时值十月，莲花已是不多见了，但公园里的其它景观还是不错的，值得一去。
还游览了古采石遗址，可谓怪石嶙峋。怎么说呢——很震撼！
站在巨石前，人类真是渺小啊。
不知道古人是如何切割和搬运这些巨石的，一定很不容易吧！

Lianhua Hill, Guangzhou - October 24, 2015

On October 24, 2015, during a business trip to Guangzhou for a financial project, I also took the opportunity to visit Lianhua Hill.
As the name suggests, Lianhua Hill is famous for its lotus flowers. However, since it was October, there weren’t many lotus flowers in bloom. Still, the other landscapes in the park were quite nice and definitely worth a visit.
I also explored the ancient Caishi Quarry site, which was full of strange, jagged rocks. How should I describe it—quite impressive!
Standing in front of these giant rocks, I couldn’t help but feel how small humans are in comparison. I wonder how the ancients were able to cut and transport these massive stones—surely it must have been incredibly difficult!
