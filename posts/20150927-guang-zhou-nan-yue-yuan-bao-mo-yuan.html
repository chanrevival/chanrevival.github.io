20150927广州南粤园宝墨园

发表于2015-09-27|更新于2025-03-03|休闲·Leisure摄影艺术·Photography
|浏览量:

2015年9月27日，小生出差广州接洽某金融项目时，顺道游览了广州的南粤园、宝墨园。
这两座园林是小生游览过的最棒的园林之一，（虽然生平也没游览过几座😁），所谓苏式园林，当如是也。
小生拍了很多照片，除了各种造景的亭台楼阁，还有各式各样的假山和工艺品。
虽然是非节假日，游客还很多。可谓是人山人海了。

Nan Yue Garden & Bao Mo Garden, Guangzhou - September 27, 2015

On September 27, 2015, during a business trip to Guangzhou for a financial project, I also took the opportunity to visit Nan Yue Garden and Bao Mo Garden.
These two gardens are among the best I’ve ever visited (although, to be honest, I haven’t seen many gardens in my life 😁). This is what a Suzhou-style garden should be like.
I took a lot of photos, including shots of the various pavilions, towers, and terraces, as well as the different types of rockeries and crafts.
Although it wasn’t a public holiday, the gardens were still very crowded. It could truly be described as a sea of people.
